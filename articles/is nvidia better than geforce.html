What Is NVIDIA?

NVIDIA is a technology company that specializes in computer graphics cards and other related products. Founded in 1993, they are one of the oldest companies in the industry and have become a leader in the field of computer graphics. They offer a wide range of products, from consumer-level GPUs to professional-grade workstation cards.

NVIDIA's GPUs are known for their performance and reliability, making them popular among gamers and professionals alike. They also offer software solutions such as CUDA, which allows developers to create applications that can take advantage of their GPUs' capabilities.

GeForce is another technology company that specializes in computer graphics cards and related products.

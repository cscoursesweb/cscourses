Introduction

An altcoin is a cryptocurrency that is an alternative to Bitcoin. It is a digital currency that uses cryptography for security and can be used to transfer funds between two parties. Altcoins are decentralized, meaning they are not controlled by any government or central authority. They are also open-source, meaning anyone can view and modify the code. Altcoins have become increasingly popular in recent years as more people become interested in cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology.

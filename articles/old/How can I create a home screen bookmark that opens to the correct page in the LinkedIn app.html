How can I create a home screen bookmark that opens to the correct page in the LinkedIn app?

Creating a home screen bookmark that opens to the correct page in the LinkedIn app is a great way to quickly access your profile and stay connected with your network. With just a few simple steps, you can create a home screen bookmark that will open to the correct page in the LinkedIn app.


The first step is to open the LinkedIn app on your device. Once the app is open, navigate to the page you want to bookmark. This could be your profile page, a group page, or any other page within the app. Once you have navigated to the page you want to bookmark, tap the “Share” icon in the top right corner of the screen.


Next, select the “Add to Home Screen” option from the list of options. This will create a shortcut to the page you are currently viewing. You can then customize the name of the bookmark and tap “Add” to save it to your home screen.


Now, whenever you tap the bookmark on your home screen, it will open directly to the page you bookmarked in the LinkedIn app. This is a great way to quickly access your profile and stay connected with your network.

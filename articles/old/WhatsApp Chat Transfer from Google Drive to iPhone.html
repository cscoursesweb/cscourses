WhatsApp Chat Transfer from Google Drive to iPhone


Are you looking for a way to transfer your WhatsApp chat from Google Drive to your iPhone? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll explain how to transfer your WhatsApp chat from Google Drive to your iPhone.


First, you’ll need to make sure that you have a Google account and that you’ve backed up your WhatsApp chat to Google Drive. To do this, open WhatsApp on your iPhone and go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup. From here, you can select “Back Up Now” to back up your chat to Google Drive.


Once you’ve backed up your chat to Google Drive, you’ll need to download the Google Drive app on your iPhone. Once you’ve done this, open the app and log in with your Google account.


Once you’ve logged in, you’ll need to find the WhatsApp chat backup file. To do this, tap the “My Drive” tab at the bottom of the screen and then tap the “WhatsApp” folder. Here, you should see the chat backup file.


Tap the chat backup file and then tap the “Download” button. This will download the file to your iPhone.


Once the file has been downloaded, open the WhatsApp app on your iPhone and go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup. From here, tap “Restore” and select the chat backup file that you just downloaded.


Your WhatsApp chat will now be transferred from Google Drive to your iPhone. You can now start using WhatsApp on your iPhone with all of your previous chat history.

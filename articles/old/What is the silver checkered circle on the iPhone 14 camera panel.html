What is the silver checkered circle on the iPhone 14 camera panel?

The silver checkered circle on the iPhone 14 camera panel is a feature known as the Apple ProRAW. This feature was first introduced with the iPhone 12 Pro and Pro Max and is now available on the iPhone 14.


Apple ProRAW is a new image format that combines the best of both worlds: the flexibility of a RAW image with the convenience of Apple’s computational photography features. It allows users to take advantage of the powerful image processing capabilities of the iPhone while still having the ability to make adjustments to the image in post-processing.


The silver checkered circle on the iPhone 14 camera panel is the visual representation of the Apple ProRAW feature. It is a reminder to users that they can take advantage of this powerful image format.


When taking a photo in Apple ProRAW, the iPhone 14 will capture a RAW image and then apply Apple’s computational photography features to it. This means that the image will have the same level of detail and dynamic range as a RAW image, but with the added benefit of Apple’s image processing algorithms.

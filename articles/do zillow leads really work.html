What Are Zillow Leads?

Zillow leads are potential buyers who have expressed an interest in a particular property on the Zillow website. These leads come from people who have either searched for a property on the site or filled out a form expressing interest in a particular listing. When someone expresses interest in a listing, they become a lead and their contact information is sent to the seller or their real estate agent.

The advantage of using Zillow leads is that they are already interested in your property and may be more likely to make an offer than someone who has not expressed any interest at all. This makes it easier for sellers to find potential buyers and increases their chances of getting an offer on their home.

How Do You Get Zillow Leads?

Getting Zillow leads is relatively easy. All you need to do is list your property on the website and wait for potential buyers to express interest in it. Once someone expresses interest, their contact information will be sent directly to you or your real estate agent so that you can follow up with them about making an offer on your home.

You can also use other methods such as advertising or social media campaigns to generate more leads from Zillow. This can help increase the number of people who see your listing and potentially express interest in it, increasing your chances of getting an offer on your home.

The short answer is yes, but there are some caveats that need to be taken into consideration when using them.

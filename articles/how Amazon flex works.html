Introduction

Amazon Flex is an on-demand delivery service that allows independent contractors to deliver packages for Amazon.com. It is a great way for people to make extra money in their spare time, as they can choose when and where they want to work. The service was launched in 2015 and has since become one of the most popular ways for people to make money on the side.

Amazon Flex works by allowing independent contractors to sign up and then select delivery blocks that fit their schedule. Once a block is selected, the contractor will be given a list of packages that need to be delivered within that block. The contractor will then pick up the packages from an Amazon warehouse or other designated location and deliver them to customers’ homes or businesses.

The amount of money earned by an Amazon Flex contractor depends on several factors, including the size of the package, the distance it needs to be delivered, and any additional fees associated with the delivery. Generally speaking, contractors can expect to earn between $18-$25 per hour depending on these factors.

How Does Amazon Flex Work?

The process of becoming an Amazon Flex contractor is relatively simple. First, you must download the app from either Google Play or Apple App Store and create an account with your personal information. Once your account has been created, you will need to provide some additional information such as your driver’s license number and vehicle information if you plan on using your own car for deliveries.

Once all of this information has been provided, you will be able to view available delivery blocks in your area and select one that fits your schedule. You will then receive a list of packages that need to be delivered within that block along with instructions on where they need to go. After picking up the packages from an Amazon warehouse or other designated location, you will then deliver them directly to customers’ homes or businesses within the allotted time frame.

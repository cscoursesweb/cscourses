Should a CNN generalize to arbitrary positions in the data?

The question of whether a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) should generalize to arbitrary positions in the data is a complex one. In order to answer this question, it is important to understand the purpose of a CNN. A CNN is a type of artificial neural network that is used for image recognition and classification. In order to do this, a CNN needs to be able to generalize to different positions in the data.

First, it allows the CNN to be able to recognize patterns in the data that may not be immediately obvious. Second, the ability of a CNN to generalize to different positions in the data allows it to be able to adapt to different types of data.

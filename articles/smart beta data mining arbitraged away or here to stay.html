What is Smart Beta Data Mining Arbitrage?

Smart beta data mining arbitrage is an investment strategy that involves taking advantage of discrepancies between different markets or asset classes. For example, if one market is trading at a higher price than another market, an investor can buy the cheaper asset and sell the more expensive one, thus making a profit from the difference in prices. This type of trading can be done manually or through automated algorithms that scan multiple markets for potential opportunities.

The goal of smart beta data mining arbitrage is to generate returns with minimal risk. By taking advantage of small discrepancies between different markets, investors can make profits without having to take on large amounts of risk. This makes it an attractive option for those who want to diversify their portfolios without taking on too much risk.

How to keep (or import) the Tor Browser's history, when updating from an older version to TBB 5.0?

Updating your Tor Browser from an older version to the latest version, Tor Browser Bundle 5.0, can be a daunting task. One of the most important things to consider when updating is how to keep or import your browser history. This article will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to keep or import your Tor Browser history when updating from an older version to TBB 5.0.


Before you begin, it is important to note that the Tor Browser Bundle 5.0 is a major update and may not be compatible with your existing browser history. Therefore, it is important to back up your existing browser history before you begin the update process.


Step 1: Back Up Your Existing Browser History


The first step in keeping or importing your Tor Browser history when updating from an older version to TBB 5.0 is to back up your existing browser history. To do this, open your Tor Browser and click on the “Tools” menu. From the drop-down menu, select “Options”. On the “Options” page, select the “Privacy” tab. Under the “History” section, click on the “Back Up” button. This will create a backup of your existing browser history.


Step 2: Download and Install Tor Browser Bundle 5.0


The next step is to download and install the Tor Browser Bundle 5.0. To do this, go to the Tor Project website and download the latest version of the Tor Browser Bundle. Once the download is complete, double-click on the file to begin the installation process. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.


Step 3: Restore Your Browser History


Once the installation is complete, you can restore your browser history. To do this, open your Tor Browser and click on the “Tools” menu. From the drop-down menu, select “Options”. On the “Options” page, select the “Privacy” tab. Under the “History” section, click on the “Restore” button. This will restore your browser history from the backup you created in Step 1.


Step 4: Enjoy Your Updated Tor Browser


Once you have restored your browser history, you can enjoy your updated Tor Browser. You should now be able to browse the web with the same level of privacy and security as before.

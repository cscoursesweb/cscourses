How can I stop Macbook Pro from decreasing screen brightness, everytime I remove the MagSafe charger?

If you’ve been having trouble with your Macbook Pro decreasing screen brightness every time you remove the MagSafe charger, you’re not alone. Many Macbook Pro users have experienced this issue, and it can be quite annoying. Fortunately, there are a few steps you can take to stop your Macbook Pro from decreasing screen brightness when you remove the MagSafe charger.


The first step is to check your Macbook Pro’s energy settings. To do this, open System Preferences and select the Energy Saver tab. Make sure that the “Automatically reduce brightness when on battery power” option is unchecked. This will prevent your Macbook Pro from automatically reducing the screen brightness when the MagSafe charger is removed.


The second step is to check your Macbook Pro’s display settings. To do this, open System Preferences and select the Displays tab. Make sure that the “Automatically adjust brightness” option is unchecked. This will prevent your Macbook Pro from automatically adjusting the screen brightness when the MagSafe charger is removed.


The third step is to check your Macbook Pro’s keyboard settings. To do this, open System Preferences and select the Keyboard tab. Make sure that the “Adjust keyboard brightness in low light” option is unchecked. This will prevent your Macbook Pro from automatically adjusting the keyboard brightness when the MagSafe charger is removed.


The fourth step is to check your Macbook Pro’s power settings. To do this, open System Preferences and select the Power tab. Make sure that the “Allow power button to turn off display” option is unchecked. This will prevent your Macbook Pro from automatically turning off the display when the MagSafe charger is removed.


Finally, if you’re still having trouble with your Macbook Pro decreasing screen brightness when the MagSafe charger is removed, you may need to reset your Macbook Pro’s SMC (System Management Controller). To do this, shut down your Macbook Pro and disconnect the MagSafe charger. Then, press and hold the power button for 10 seconds. This will reset the SMC and should stop your Macbook Pro from decreasing screen brightness when the MagSafe charger is removed.

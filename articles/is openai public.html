One of the main benefits of open source artificial intelligence technology is increased collaboration between researchers from different fields since they can easily share their work with each other without having to worry about copyright issues or licensing fees. Additionally, open source AI technology can help reduce costs associated with developing new AI applications since developers don't have to pay licensing fees or royalties for using these technologies which can save companies money in the long run.

Tesla Model 3: The Most Affordable Option

The Tesla Model 3 is the most affordable option in the range and starts at around $35,000. It has a range of up to 310 miles on a single charge and can accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 3.5 seconds. It also has Autopilot capabilities that allow for semi-autonomous driving. Inside, you’ll find a 15-inch touchscreen display with access to all your favorite apps and streaming services. The Tesla Model S is a step up in luxury from the Model 3 but still relatively affordable at around $75,000. Tesla Model X: The Most Expensive Option

The Tesla Model X is the most expensive option in the range but still relatively affordable at around $90,000. It has an impressive range of up to 325 miles on a single charge and can accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 2.9 seconds – making it one of the fastest SUVs on the market today! Inside, you’ll find an even larger 17-inch touchscreen display with access to all your favorite apps and streaming services as well as heated seats and dual zone climate control for added comfort.

In Keynote, how to change no-fill color from black to something else?

If you’re looking to change the no-fill color from black to something else in Keynote, you’ve come to the right place. Keynote is a powerful presentation software from Apple that allows you to create stunning presentations with ease. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps of changing the no-fill color from black to something else in Keynote.


The first step is to open the Keynote presentation you want to edit. Once you’ve opened the presentation, select the shape or object you want to change the no-fill color of. You can do this by clicking on the shape or object with your mouse.


Once you’ve selected the shape or object, click on the “Format” tab at the top of the screen. This will open up a menu of options for you to choose from. In the “Fill” section, you’ll see a drop-down menu with the current no-fill color. Click on the drop-down menu and select the color you want to use.


Once you’ve selected the color, click on the “Apply” button at the bottom of the menu. This will apply the new color to the shape or object you selected. You can also click on the “Preview” button to see how the new color will look before you apply it.

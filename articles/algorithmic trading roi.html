What Is Algorithmic Trading ROI?

Algorithmic trading ROI is the return on investment that an investor can expect from using an algorithmic trading system. This return is calculated by taking the total profits generated by the system over a given period of time and dividing it by the total amount invested in the system. The result is expressed as a percentage. For example, if an investor invests $10,000 in an algorithmic trading system and earns $1,000 in profits over a one-year period, then their algorithmic trading ROI would be 10%.

It is important to note that algorithmic trading ROI does not take into account any fees or commissions associated with using the system. These fees can significantly reduce your overall returns so it is important to factor them into your calculations when determining your expected returns from algorithmic trading.

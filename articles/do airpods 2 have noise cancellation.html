What Is Noise Cancellation?

Noise cancellation is a technology that uses sound waves to reduce or eliminate unwanted background noise. It works by creating an “anti-noise” wave that cancels out the unwanted sound waves. This technology is used in many products such as headphones, earbuds, and even some cars.

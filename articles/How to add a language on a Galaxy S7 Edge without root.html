How to add a language on a Galaxy S7 Edge without root?

Adding a language to your Samsung Galaxy S7 Edge without root is a relatively simple process. With the right steps, you can easily add a new language to your device and start using it right away. Here’s how to do it:


1. Open the Settings app on your Galaxy S7 Edge.


2. Scroll down and tap on the “Language and Input” option.


3. Tap on the “Add Language” option.


4. Select the language you want to add from the list of available languages.


5. Tap on the “Download” button to download the language pack.


6. Once the language pack is downloaded, tap on the “Activate” button to activate the language.


7. You will now be able to use the language on your Galaxy S7 Edge.

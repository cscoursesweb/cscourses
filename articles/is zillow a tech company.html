What Is Zillow?

Zillow is an online real estate marketplace that allows users to search for homes and compare prices. It also provides tools for buyers and sellers to connect with each other and make informed decisions about their real estate transactions. The company also offers mortgage calculators, home value estimates, rental listings, and other services related to buying and selling homes.

How Does Zillow Use Technology?

Zillow uses technology in many ways to provide its services. For example, its website uses algorithms to generate home value estimates based on data from public records and user-submitted information. It also uses machine learning algorithms to match buyers with sellers based on their preferences. Additionally, its mobile app allows users to search for homes on the go and access other features such as mortgage calculators and rental listings.

What Makes Zillow a Tech Company?

At its core, Zillow is a tech company because it relies heavily on technology to provide its services. Its website and mobile app are powered by algorithms that allow users to quickly find what they’re looking for without having to manually search through thousands of listings or calculate complex formulas themselves. Additionally, its use of machine learning algorithms makes it easier for buyers and sellers to connect with each other based on their preferences.

What Is Algorithmic Trading?

Algorithmic trading is a form of automated stock market trading that uses computer algorithms to determine when to buy and sell stocks. The algorithms are programmed with specific criteria such as price movements, volume levels, or other technical indicators. When these criteria are met, the algorithm will automatically execute a trade on behalf of the trader. This type of automated trading eliminates the need for manual intervention and allows traders to take advantage of market opportunities quickly and efficiently.

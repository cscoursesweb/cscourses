How do I re-enable drag and drop (previously working) for Tor Browser 12?

Re-enabling drag and drop for Tor Browser 12 is a relatively simple process. Drag and drop is a feature that allows you to quickly move files, folders, and other items from one location to another. It is a useful feature that can save you time and effort when transferring files.


Fortunately, re-enabling drag and drop for Tor Browser 12 is a straightforward process. Here are the steps you need to take to get it working again:


1. Open Tor Browser 12 and click on the “Tools” menu.


2. Select “Options” from the drop-down menu.


3. In the “General” tab, scroll down to the “Drag and Drop” section.


4. Check the box next to “Enable Drag and Drop”.


5. Click “OK” to save your changes.


Once you have completed these steps, drag and drop should be re-enabled for Tor Browser 12. You should now be able to move files, folders, and other items from one location to another with ease.


It is important to note that drag and drop may not work in all cases. For example, if you are using a third-party application that does not support drag and drop, it may not work. Additionally, some websites may not allow you to drag and drop items.


If you are having trouble re-enabling drag and drop for Tor Browser 12, you may need to update your browser. You can do this by clicking on the “Help” menu and selecting “Check for Updates”. This will check for any available updates and install them if necessary.

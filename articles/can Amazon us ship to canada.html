Yes, there are additional fees and taxes that may apply when shipping items from Amazon US to Canada. These include customs duties (which vary depending on the item being shipped), GST/HST (Goods & Services Tax/Harmonized Sales Tax), PST (Provincial Sales Tax), brokerage fees (which vary depending on the carrier used) and fuel surcharges (which also vary depending on the carrier used). It’s important to note that these fees are not included in the cost of shipping and must be paid separately upon delivery of your package(s).

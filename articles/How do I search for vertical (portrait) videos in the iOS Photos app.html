How do I search for vertical (portrait) videos in the iOS Photos app?

Searching for vertical (portrait) videos in the iOS Photos app is a simple process. The Photos app on iOS devices allows you to search for videos based on orientation, making it easy to find the vertical videos you’re looking for. Here’s how to do it:


1. Open the Photos app on your iOS device.


2. Tap the “Albums” tab at the bottom of the screen.


3. Scroll down and tap “Videos”.


4. Tap the “Orientation” tab at the top of the screen.


5. Select “Portrait” from the list of options.


This will filter your videos to only show those that are in portrait orientation. You can also select “Landscape” to view videos in landscape orientation.


You can also search for vertical videos using the search bar at the top of the screen. Simply type in “portrait” or “vertical” and the Photos app will show you all the videos that match your search query.


Once you’ve found the video you’re looking for, you can tap on it to view it in full screen. You can also share the video with friends and family, or save it to your device for later viewing.

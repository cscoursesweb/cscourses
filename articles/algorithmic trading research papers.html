What Is Algorithmic Trading?

Algorithmic trading is a type of automated trading that uses computer algorithms to make decisions about when to buy and sell financial instruments. It is based on the idea that computers can be programmed to identify patterns in market data and then use those patterns to make decisions about when to enter or exit trades. Algorithmic trading has become increasingly popular in recent years due to its ability to quickly identify profitable opportunities in the markets.

Types of Algorithmic Trading Strategies

There are many different types of algorithmic trading strategies that can be used by traders. Some common strategies include trend following, mean reversion, arbitrage, and market making. Each strategy has its own set of advantages and disadvantages, so it is important for traders to understand which strategy best suits their needs before they begin using it.

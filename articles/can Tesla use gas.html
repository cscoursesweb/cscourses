Now that we know what a Tesla is, let's answer our original question: Can you use gas in a Tesla? The short answer is yes, but only under certain circumstances. While most Teslas are designed to run solely on electricity, some models do have an option for using gasoline as well.

The most common way to use gasoline in a Tesla is through an aftermarket conversion kit known as "Range Extender." This kit allows you to install an internal combustion engine (ICE) into your vehicle which will generate electricity for your battery pack when needed. However, it should be noted that installing an ICE into your vehicle will significantly reduce its efficiency and range compared to running solely on electricity alone. Therefore, it's important to consider all of these factors before deciding if using gasoline in your Tesla is right for you.

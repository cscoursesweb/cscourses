What is Volatility Managed Investing?

Volatility managed investing is a strategy that seeks to reduce risk by diversifying investments across a range of asset classes. This type of investing involves actively managing the portfolio’s exposure to different asset classes in order to minimize losses and maximize gains. The goal is to achieve greater returns with less risk than traditional buy-and-hold strategies.

The key components of volatility managed investing include: diversification, active management, and risk management. Diversification involves spreading investments across multiple asset classes in order to reduce the overall risk of the portfolio. Active management involves regularly monitoring and adjusting the portfolio’s exposure in order to take advantage of market opportunities and minimize losses. Risk management involves setting limits on how much money can be invested in each asset class in order to protect against large losses.

What is Algorithmic Trading?

Algorithmic trading is a form of automated stock market trading that uses computer algorithms to make decisions about when and how much stock should be bought or sold. The algorithms are programmed with specific rules that determine when trades should be made based on market conditions such as price movements, volume levels, news events, etc. Algorithmic traders use these rules-based systems to make decisions quickly and accurately without having to manually analyze the markets themselves.

Algorithmic trading has become increasingly popular in recent years due to its ability to execute trades faster than manual traders can. This speed allows algorithmic traders to capitalize on short-term price movements that manual traders may miss out on. Additionally, algorithmic traders can backtest their strategies against historical data before putting them into practice in real-time markets.

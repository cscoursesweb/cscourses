Why do dual-SIM smart phones always have to have 1 SIM card turned on?

Dual-SIM smart phones are becoming increasingly popular, as they allow users to have two phone numbers on one device. However, many people are confused as to why dual-SIM phones always have to have one SIM card turned on.


The reason for this is that dual-SIM phones are designed to use two different networks simultaneously. This means that one SIM card is used for making calls and sending texts, while the other is used for data services such as browsing the internet or using apps.


In order for the phone to be able to use both networks, it needs to have one SIM card turned on at all times. This is because the phone needs to be able to switch between the two networks in order to provide the best coverage and connection.


If both SIM cards were turned off, the phone would not be able to switch between networks and would be unable to make calls or access the internet. This would mean that the phone would be unable to perform any of its basic functions.


It is also important to note that dual-SIM phones are designed to be used with two different networks. This means that if both SIM cards are from the same network, the phone will not be able to switch between them and will only be able to use one network at a time.

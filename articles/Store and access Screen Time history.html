Store and access Screen Time history?

Screen Time is a feature on Apple devices that allows users to monitor and limit the amount of time they spend on their devices. It also provides detailed reports on how much time is spent on each app and website. With this feature, users can set limits on the amount of time they spend on certain apps and websites, as well as set time limits for their children.


But what if you want to store and access your Screen Time history? Fortunately, Apple makes it easy to do so. Here’s how you can store and access your Screen Time history.


First, open the Settings app on your device. Then, tap on Screen Time. You’ll see a list of options, including “View Screen Time Report.” Tap on this option to view your Screen Time report.


The report will show you how much time you’ve spent on each app and website, as well as the total amount of time you’ve spent on your device. You can also view the report for each day, week, or month.


If you want to store your Screen Time report, you can do so by tapping on the “Share Report” button at the bottom of the report. This will allow you to save the report as a PDF file, which you can then store on your device or share with others.


If you want to access your Screen Time report at a later date, you can do so by opening the Settings app and tapping on Screen Time. Then, tap on “View Screen Time Report” and you’ll be able to view your report.

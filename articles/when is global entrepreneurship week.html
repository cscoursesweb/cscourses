Introduction

Global Entrepreneurship Week (GEW) is an international celebration of the innovators and job creators who launch startups that bring ideas to life, drive economic growth and expand human welfare. It is a platform for people everywhere to connect with potential collaborators, mentors and even investors, launching their ideas into action. GEW takes place every November in over 170 countries around the world. It is a time for entrepreneurs to come together to share their stories, learn from each other and celebrate the power of entrepreneurship.

GEW was founded in 2008 by the Kauffman Foundation and has since grown into a global movement that celebrates innovation and encourages collaboration between entrepreneurs, investors, educators, policy makers and other stakeholders. The goal of GEW is to inspire people everywhere to take their ideas from concept to reality by connecting them with resources they need to succeed.

When Is Global Entrepreneurship Week?

Global Entrepreneurship Week takes place annually in November. This year it will be held from November 16th-22nd 2020. During this week, entrepreneurs around the world will come together virtually or in person (depending on local regulations) to celebrate innovation and collaboration.

The week-long event includes activities such as hackathons, pitch competitions, workshops, networking events and more. These activities are designed to help entrepreneurs develop their skills, find mentors or investors and connect with like-minded individuals who can help them take their ideas forward.

What Are The Benefits Of Participating In Global Entrepreneurship Week?

Participating in GEW can be beneficial for both aspiring entrepreneurs as well as established business owners. For aspiring entrepreneurs it provides an opportunity to learn about entrepreneurship from experienced professionals while also connecting with potential mentors or investors who can help them take their ideas forward. For established business owners it provides an opportunity to network with other business owners as well as potential customers or partners who can help them grow their businesses further.

Additionally, participating in GEW can also provide access to resources such as funding opportunities or grants that can help entrepreneurs get started on their journey towards success. Finally, participating in GEW also helps spread awareness about entrepreneurship which can lead to more people taking the plunge into starting their own businesses which ultimately leads to economic growth for everyone involved!

Amazon Fire TV Cube

The last type of Amazon Fire Stick that I’ll be discussing is the Amazon Fire TV Cube. This model combines the features of both the Lite and 4K models into one device. It has access to 4K content and Dolby Atmos audio as well as a voice-controlled remote control for easy searching. The main downside of this model is that it doesn’t have access to some popular streaming services like HBO Max or Disney+.

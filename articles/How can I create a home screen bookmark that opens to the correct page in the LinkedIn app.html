Creating a Home Screen Bookmark that Opens to the Correct Page in the LinkedIn App


If you’re a frequent user of the LinkedIn app, you may want to create a home screen bookmark that opens to the correct page in the app. This can be a great way to quickly access the page you need without having to search for it. Here’s how to create a home screen bookmark that opens to the correct page in the LinkedIn app.


First, open the LinkedIn app on your device. Once the app is open, navigate to the page you want to create a bookmark for. For example, if you want to create a bookmark for your profile page, navigate to your profile page.


Once you’re on the page you want to bookmark, tap the three dots in the top right corner of the screen. This will open a menu with several options. Tap the “Add to Home Screen” option.


This will open a new window with a few options. You can choose to name the bookmark, or you can leave the default name. You can also choose an icon for the bookmark. Once you’ve made your selections, tap the “Add” button.


The bookmark will now appear on your home screen. When you tap the bookmark, it will open the LinkedIn app and take you directly to the page you bookmarked.

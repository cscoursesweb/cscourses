I added my client to WhatsApp, now I get messages from him at 11pm. How to act?

If you've recently added a client to your WhatsApp contacts list, you may be feeling a bit overwhelmed by the sudden influx of messages coming in at all hours of the day and night. It can be difficult to know how to act when you're receiving messages from a client at 11pm, especially if you're not used to communicating with them outside of normal business hours.


The first thing to consider is whether or not the client is expecting a response from you. If they are, then you should respond as soon as possible. However, if the message is more of a casual check-in or a friendly chat, then you may want to wait until the morning to respond. This will give you time to think about your response and ensure that you're providing the best possible customer service.


If you're not sure whether or not the client is expecting a response, it's best to err on the side of caution and respond. Even if the message is more of a casual check-in, it's still important to show that you value their business and are available to help.


When responding to a client at 11pm, it's important to keep your response professional and courteous. Avoid using slang or informal language, and make sure to use proper grammar and spelling. You should also be mindful of the time difference between you and the client, as this could affect the response time.


Finally, it's important to remember that you don't have to be available 24/7. If you're feeling overwhelmed by the influx of messages, it's okay to set boundaries and let the client know that you're not available after a certain time. This will help to ensure that you're providing the best possible customer service while also protecting your own mental health.

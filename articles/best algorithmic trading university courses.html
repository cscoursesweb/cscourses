What is Algorithmic Trading?

Algorithmic trading is a type of automated trading that uses computer algorithms to make decisions about when and how to trade financial instruments. It is used by professional traders and investors who want to take advantage of market opportunities quickly and efficiently. Algorithmic trading can be used in any market, from stocks and bonds to commodities and currencies.

The main benefit of algorithmic trading is that it allows traders to make decisions quickly without having to manually analyze data or make complex calculations. This makes it possible for traders to take advantage of short-term market movements that would otherwise be difficult or impossible for humans to detect. Additionally, algorithmic trading can help reduce risk by limiting exposure to certain markets or strategies.

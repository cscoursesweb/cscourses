Top 5 best switches for gaming in 2023

BUY BUTTON

Cherry MX Red Switches

Cherry MX Red switches are linear switches, meaning they have a smooth and consistent keystroke without any tactile bump or audible click. This makes them ideal for fast-paced gaming and typing. The switches are also known for their low actuation force, which requires very little effort to press a key.

Advantages: Ideal for fast-paced gaming and typing Smooth and consistent keystroke Low actuation force

Disadvantages: Lack of tactile feedback No audible click

Cherry MX Brown Switches

Cherry MX Brown switches are a popular choice among users who want a balance between gaming and typing. They offer a slight tactile bump when the key is pressed, giving users a little bit of feedback as they type. The actuation force is still relatively low, making them a good choice for those who want a responsive keyboard.

Advantages: Good balance between gaming and typing Slight tactile bump Low actuation force

Disadvantages: Slightly more resistance than Cherry MX Red switches No audible click

Cherry MX Blue Switches

Cherry MX Blue switches are the perfect choice for typists who want a keyboard that provides a satisfying click with every keystroke. The switches offer a tactile bump and an audible click, making them ideal for users who want a keyboard that gives them a good amount of feedback. The actuation force is slightly higher than the Red and Brown switches, but it's still manageable for most users.

Advantages: Ideal for typists who want a satisfying click with every keystroke Tactile bump and audible click

Disadvantages: Higher actuation force than Red and Brown switches Noisier than other switches

Kailh Red/Brown/Blue Switches

Kailh switches are a budget-friendly alternative to Cherry MX switches. They offer a similar typing experience to Cherry MX switches, but at a lower price point. Kailh Red, Brown, and Blue switches are similar to their Cherry MX counterparts and provide a good balance between gaming and typing.

Advantages: Budget-friendly alternative to Cherry MX switches Similar typing experience to Cherry MX switches

Disadvantages: Quality may not be as good as Cherry MX switches Not as widely available as Cherry MX switches

Gateron Red/Brown/Blue Switches

Gateron switches are another budget-friendly alternative to Cherry MX switches. Like Kailh switches, they offer a similar typing experience to Cherry MX switches, but at a lower price point. Gateron Red, Brown, and Blue switches are similar to their Cherry MX counterparts and provide a good balance between gaming and typing.

Advantages: Budget-friendly alternative to Cherry MX switches Similar typing experience to Cherry MX switches

Disadvantages: Quality may not be as good as Cherry MX switches Not as widely available as Cherry MX switches

In conclusion, the best keyboard switch for you will depend on your personal preferences and what you're using the keyboard for. If you're a gamer, you may prefer Cherry MX Red switches.
